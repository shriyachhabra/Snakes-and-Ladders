INSTRUCTIONS
Duration : 60 Min
No. of team members per team : 1-2
Highest possible level : 16

1> Each team will be given questions to solve. The team will be promoted by 2 levels for each correct answer and will be demoted
by 1 level for an incorrect answer.

2> Score will be given according to the no. of levels promoted or demoted.
(for eg. +2 for each correct ans and -1 for an incorrect ans)

3> There will be no negative marking for the first question or in
case the team reaches the 0th level

4> Levels 13,14,15 and 16 will have coding questions. Players
will be promoted/ demoted by levels +2, +1 or -1 according to the number of test cases passed for each question.

5> The team that reaches the maximum level in the minimum time wins.
